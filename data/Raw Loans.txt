======Maha Super Flexi Housing Loan Scheme======
Core Idea
This loan is linked with a savings account. Any surplus amount deposited in the account reduces the interest burden on the loan. The borrower has the flexibility to withdraw the surplus funds anytime, making it a flexible repayment system.
Features
The loan is connected to a flexi savings account. The surplus balance in the account is used to offset the loan amount for interest calculation. Interest is calculated on a daily reducing balance. The net balance is considered as loan amount minus savings balance. The account provides full banking access including cheque facility, net banking, and mobile banking.
Benefits
The scheme provides liquidity as funds can be withdrawn at any time. It helps in saving interest on the loan. It offers flexibility in repayment and fund management.
Eligibility
The scheme is available for salaried individuals, self-employed individuals, businessmen, and agriculturists.
Loan Details
The minimum loan amount is 50 lakh rupees. There is no maximum loan limit. The repayment tenure can go up to 30 years or until the borrower reaches 75 years of age.
Interest Rate
The interest rate is slightly higher than the regular housing loan rate, typically by 0.25 percent.
Processing Fee
The processing fee is 0.25 percent of the loan amount, subject to a maximum of 25,000 rupees. In some cases, government employees may receive a waiver.
======Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Urban 2.0======
Objective
The scheme aims to provide affordable housing to urban households belonging to economically weaker section, low income group, and middle income group categories. It supports individuals in purchasing or constructing houses with government-backed interest subsidy.
Income Eligibility
Economically weaker section includes households with annual income up to 3 lakh rupees. Low income group includes households with annual income between 3 lakh and 6 lakh rupees. Middle income group includes households with annual income up to 9 lakh rupees. 
Loan and Property Limits
The maximum loan amount allowed under the scheme is 25 lakh rupees. The maximum property value allowed is 35 lakh rupees. The maximum carpet area permitted is 120 square meters. 
Ownership Condition
The applicant or any family member must not own a pucca house anywhere in India. A declaration is required to confirm this condition. 
Key Conditions
Aadhaar is mandatory for all beneficiaries. Applicants must provide a self-undertaking as proof of eligibility. Beneficiaries are required to register through the official portal to avail the scheme benefits. 
Interest Subsidy
An interest subsidy of 4 percent is provided on the first 8 lakh of the loan amount for a tenure of up to 12 years. The maximum subsidy benefit can go up to 1.80 lakh rupees. The subsidy is released in installments and credited to the loan account. 
Eligibility Conditions
Applicants must belong to eligible income categories and should not own a house. The property must be located in statutory towns or notified urban areas. 
Application Process
The applicant first applies for a home loan through the bank. Then the applicant applies for the interest subsidy under the scheme. After verification, the subsidy amount is credited directly to the loan account. 
======Gold Loan======
Overview
Gold loan is a secured loan where the borrower pledges gold jewellery or ornaments to obtain funds. It allows individuals to use their gold assets without selling them and provides quick access to money with minimal documentation. 
Purpose
The loan can be used for personal needs, business requirements, emergencies, education, or any financial requirement. 
Interest Rate
The interest rate starts from approximately 8.50 percent per annum depending on the loan type and amount. 
Loan Amount
The minimum loan amount is 20,000 rupees. The maximum loan amount can go up to 100 lakh rupees depending on the value of the gold pledged. 
Tenure
The maximum tenure for the loan is up to 24 months. In case of bullet repayment, the tenure can be up to 12 months. 
Repayment Options
The loan can be repaid through equated monthly installments or through bullet repayment where the principal and interest are paid at the end of the tenure. 
Eligibility
All individuals including bank staff can apply for the loan. The applicant must be at least 18 years old and should own the gold being pledged. Basic KYC requirements must be fulfilled. 
Security
The loan is secured against gold jewellery or ornaments. Loans are not granted against bullion or primary gold. 
Loan to Value Ratio
The loan amount is generally up to 75 percent of the value of the gold pledged. 
Processing Charges
Processing charges vary based on the loan amount. There are no charges for smaller loan amounts, and nominal fees are applied for higher loan slabs. 
Additional Charges
There are no prepayment or foreclosure charges. Documentation and inspection charges are also not applicable. 
Features
The loan offers quick processing and fast disbursal. It provides flexible loan amounts based on gold value. The pledged gold is stored securely by the bank. Multiple repayment options are available to suit different financial situations. 
Documents Required
Applicants need identity proof such as Aadhaar, PAN, passport, or driving license. Address proof such as electricity bill, Aadhaar, or passport is required. Passport size photographs are also needed. 
======Education Loan======
Overview
Education loan is provided to support students in pursuing higher education in India and abroad. It helps cover academic expenses and enables students to achieve their career goals through financial assistance. 
Interest Rate
The interest rate starts from approximately 6.85 percent per annum depending on the scheme and eligibility. 
Loan Schemes
The bank offers multiple education loan schemes such as Maha Scholar Education Loan for premier institutions, Model Education Loan for studies in India and abroad, Maha Bank Skill Loan for skill-based courses, and Maha Scholar Overseas Loan for foreign education. 
Features
The loan offers low EMI options and higher loan amounts. It provides simplified disbursement and allows tracking of loan status. There are no hidden charges and no prepayment penalty. In some cases, up to 100 percent financing is available for premier institutions and no collateral is required. 
Benefits
The loan covers tuition fees, hostel charges, cost of books, and other academic expenses. It provides financial support for both domestic and international education. Interest concessions are available for girl students and meritorious candidates. 
Eligibility
Students who are Indian nationals and have secured admission to recognized institutions in India or abroad are eligible. A co-applicant such as a parent or guardian is usually required. 
Loan Amount and Security
For loans up to 7.5 lakh rupees, no collateral is required. For higher amounts, collateral security may be required. Under certain schemes for premier institutions, collateral may not be required even for higher loan amounts. 
Repayment
The repayment tenure can extend up to 15 years. Repayment usually begins after a moratorium period, which is either one year after course completion or six months after getting a job, whichever is earlier. 
Processing Fee
There is no processing fee for certain schemes like loans for premier institutions. For other schemes, a small processing fee may be applicable, especially for studies abroad. 
Documents Required
Applicants need academic records, proof of admission, expense details, identity and address proof, photographs, bank statements, and income proof of the co-applicant. 
Interest Subsidy
Government-backed subsidy schemes are available such as Central Sector Interest Subsidy Scheme, Padho Pardesh Scheme, and Dr Ambedkar Scheme for eligible categories. 
======Personal Loan======
Overview
Personal loan is an unsecured loan that can be used for various personal expenses such as medical emergencies, travel, education, weddings, or other financial needs. It does not require any collateral and is processed with minimal documentation. 
Interest Rate
The interest rate starts from approximately 8.75 percent per annum depending on the applicant’s profile and eligibility. 
Loan Amount
The loan amount can go up to 20 lakh rupees. Typically, the eligible loan amount is up to 20 times the borrower’s gross monthly income. 
Tenure
The repayment tenure generally ranges up to 60 months for most categories. In some cases, such as salary account holders with the bank, tenure may extend up to 84 months. 
Eligibility
Applicants must have a minimum annual income of 3 lakh rupees. The loan is available for salaried individuals, professionals, and business class customers. No guarantor is required in most cases. 
Features
The loan offers low EMI options and higher loan amounts. It provides quick approval and simplified disbursement. Minimal documentation is required. There are no hidden charges and no prepayment penalty. 
Processing Fee
The processing fee is approximately 1 percent of the loan amount plus applicable taxes. 
Documents Required
Applicants need identity proof, bank statements, income proof, and other relevant documents depending on the category of loan. 
Repayment
The loan is repaid through equated monthly installments which are auto-debited from the borrower’s bank account.
